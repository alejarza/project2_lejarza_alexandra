Design Portfolio

The design classes have concentrated on Adobe Creative Suite, specifically InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop. I created realistic mockups for websites using Adobe XD. I also learned theory on user experience. I took a critical look at existing sites and products and made recommendations for better design and usability.
